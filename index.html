We offer three specialized transport services across East and Central Africa.

Our services include: Dry Container Transit (200+ trucks), Bulk Cargo Tippers (100+ trucks), and Local Shunting Operations (40 trucks). We serve Kenya, Uganda, Rwanda, Tanzania, DRC, and South Sudan with our modern fleet of 300+ vehicles.

We provide 24/7 real-time tracking for all shipments.

You can track your shipment using our online tracking system by entering your tracking number. Our advanced GPS technology provides real-time updates on your cargo's location and estimated delivery time. You can also call our customer service team at +254 723 339 000 for assistance.

Over 20 years of experience with guaranteed on-time delivery and modern fleet.

Since 2002, we've built a reputation for reliability and excellence. Our advantages include: 300+ modern trucks, 24/7 tracking, comprehensive insurance coverage, experienced drivers, flexible payment options, and strong partnerships across the region. We guarantee on-time delivery and provide round-the-clock customer support.

Getting a quote is quick and easy through multiple channels.
