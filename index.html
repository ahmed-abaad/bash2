About Bash Hauliers

East Africa's Leading Transport & Logistics Partner Since 2002

From our humble beginnings as a family-owned business, Bash Hauliers has evolved into a regional powerhouse in transport and logistics. We specialize in comprehensive cargo transportation solutions across East and Central Africa, operating a modern fleet of 300+ trucks with unwavering commitment to reliability, efficiency, and customer satisfaction.
