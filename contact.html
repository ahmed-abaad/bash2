Frequently Asked Questions

Quick Answers

Find answers to commonly asked questions about our services, processes, and policies.

How quickly can you provide a transport quote?
We provide detailed quotes within 2 hours during business hours. For urgent requests, call us directly at +254 723 339 000 for immediate assistance. Our online quote form ensures we have all necessary details to provide accurate pricing.
What countries do you serve?
We provide transport services across 6 countries: Kenya, Uganda, Rwanda, Tanzania, Democratic Republic of Congo (DRC), and South Sudan. Our extensive network covers all major cities and destinations in these regions.
Do you provide cargo insurance?
Yes, we offer comprehensive cargo insurance coverage for all shipments. Insurance can be arranged at competitive rates and provides protection against loss, damage, or theft during transit. This service can be added to your quote request.
Can I track my shipment in real-time?
All our vehicles are equipped with GPS tracking systems. Clients receive tracking access through our client portal and can monitor their shipments in real-time, including location updates and estimated arrival times.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept various payment methods including bank transfers, cash payments, and for established clients, we offer credit terms. Payment terms are typically 30 days for corporate clients with approved credit facilities.
