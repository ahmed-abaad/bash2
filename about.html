Our Story

From Family Vision to Regional Leadership

In 2002, the Bash family embarked on a journey with a simple yet powerful vision: to provide reliable, efficient transport solutions that would connect East Africa's growing economies. What started as a small family-owned business with just a handful of trucks has evolved into the region's most trusted logistics partner.

Our founders recognized the critical role that efficient transportation plays in economic development. They understood that businesses needed a partner they could rely on - one that would treat their cargo with the same care and urgency as their own.

Today, Bash Hauliers stands as a testament to what vision, hard work, and unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction can achieve. We've grown from a local transport company to a regional powerhouse, but our core values remain unchanged.

Our Journey

Milestones of Excellence

Key moments that shaped Bash Hauliers into East Africa's leading transport and logistics company.

  • 2002 - The Beginning

    Bash Hauliers was founded as a family-owned business with 5 trucks, focusing on local container transportation in Mombasa. Our commitment to reliability and customer service quickly set us apart.

  • 2005 - Fleet Expansion

    Expanded our fleet to 25 trucks and began serving routes to Nairobi and other major Kenyan cities. Introduced our first GPS tracking systems for enhanced cargo security.

  • 2008 - Regional Expansion

    Launched cross-border operations to Uganda and Tanzania. Established strategic partnerships with regional logistics companies and achieved 50+ truck milestone.

  • 2012 - Industry Recognition

    Became members of Kenya Transport Association and Kenya Shippers Council. Received our first industry excellence award for outstanding service delivery.

  • 2015 - Service Diversification

    Launched Bash Contractors Ltd for earth-moving equipment hire. Introduced specialized bulk cargo tippers and expanded to Rwanda and DRC markets.

  • 2018 - Technology Integration

    Implemented advanced fleet management systems and real-time cargo tracking. Launched our digital platform for enhanced customer experience and transparency.

  • 2020 - 300+ Fleet Milestone

    Achieved our 300+ truck milestone and expanded operations to DRC and South Sudan. Maintained 99.5% on-time delivery rate despite global challenges.

  • 2024 - Future Ready

    Leading East Africa's logistics transformation with sustainable practices, AI-powered route optimization, and continued expansion across the region.
