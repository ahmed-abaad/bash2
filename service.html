Comprehensive Logistics Solutions

Each service category is designed with specific expertise and equipment to ensure optimal performance for different cargo types and transportation needs.

Dry Container Transit

Our flagship service featuring 200+ state-of-the-art trucks exclusively dedicated to secure and timely dry container transportation across East and Central Africa.

Key Features:
  • Mercedes & FAW premium trucks
  • Real-time GPS tracking
  • 24/7 monitoring & support
  • Comprehensive insurance
  • Cross-border expertise
Ideal For:

• Import/Export containers

• Manufactured goods

• Consumer products

• Time-sensitive cargo

Bulk Cargo Tippers

100+ specially engineered tippers designed for efficient transportation of bulk cargo, serving both local distribution and long-haul transit routes.

Key Features:
  • High-capacity tippers
  • Specialized bulk handling
  • Experienced operators
  • Flexible scheduling
  • Load optimization

• Construction materials

• Agricultural products

• Mining materials

Local Shunting Operations

40 dedicated trucks on standby for swift container movement from Container Freight Stations (CFS) to secure yards and vice versa.

  • Rapid response times
  • CFS to yard transfers
  • Secure handling protocols
  • Port coordination
  • Same-day service
Ideal For:

• Port operations

• Container repositioning

• Warehouse transfers

• Emergency movements
